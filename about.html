Welcome!

A warm welcome to Sweetheart Embroidery Online! Our team is dedicated to providing you with the most exquisite and creative stitch patterns for your crafting pleasure. We're thrilled that you've chosen to join us on this creative journey!

At Sweetheart Embroidery Online, our founder Linda, is not only passionate about all things stitching but also a crafting enthusiast, mother, and textile aficionado. Linda embarked on this stitchy adventure years ago with the mission to share her passion for needlework, machine embroidery and to help crafters like you bring their creative visions to life.

Our stitch patterns have gained recognition in the crafting world and have given joy to thousands of enthusiasts. Now we invite you to explore our wonderful blog, where you can find a treasure trove of interesting topics for all your stitching needs, whether you prefer to stitch by hand or by machine. Dive into a world of stitching tips, project inspiration, embroidery history and expert advice that will elevate your crafting skills to new heights.

We're here to save you time, ignite your creative spirit, and be your go-to resource for all things stitching. Thank you for choosing Sweetheart Embroidery Online as your crafting companion.

With love,

Linda and the stitching team
